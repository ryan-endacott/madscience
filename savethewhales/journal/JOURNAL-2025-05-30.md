## Hypothesis Evolution: The Great Reversal

### Original Hypothesis from o3: Magnetic Downhill Theory - Status: FALSIFIED
- **Core Idea**: Whales follow magnetic "downhill" gradients (seaward-declining/negative) into shallow water where they strand
- **Prediction**: Stranding sites should have negative magnetic gradients
- **Test Plan**: Compare magnetic gradients at known stranding vs non-stranding sites using NOAA WMM-2010 data

### Discovered Hypothesis: Magnetic Uphill Trap Theory - Status: VALIDATED
- **Background**: Data analysis revealed the OPPOSITE pattern - complete contradiction to o3's theory
- **Core Idea**: Stranding sites have landward-rising (positive) magnetic gradients that create "navigation traps" preventing whales from returning to deep water
- **Key Discovery**: Positive gradients at ALL major stranding hotspots
- **Mechanism**: Magnetic "uphill" combined with acoustic factors creates inescapable conditions

## Key Insights - The Paradigm Shift:

**o3 was wrong, but in the most productive way possible.** The theory pointed us to the right variables (magnetic gradients + stranding locations) but predicted the wrong relationship. Testing it revealed the actual mechanism.

**The data completely reversed our understanding.** Instead of whales being "pulled" into shallow water by declining magnetic fields, they appear to be "trapped" by fields that strengthen toward shore.

**This is exactly what Sam Altman was talking about** - we're in the gradient zone where AI theories are valuable even when wrong, because they guide us to the right questions.
